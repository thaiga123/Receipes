1. Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice of bread
  • ½ avocado
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: chili flakes, sliced tomato, or a drizzle of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash the avocado and spread it on the toast.
  3. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and any additional toppings.

2. Banana Peanut Butter Wrap

Ingredients

  • 1 tortilla
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • Optional: a drizzle of honey or chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Spread peanut butter over the tortilla.
  2. Place the banana in the center and roll the tortilla around it.
  3. Slice into bite-sized pieces or eat as a wrap.

3. Quick Veggie Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed greens or lettuce
  • ½ cucumber, sliced
  • 1 small carrot, shredded
  • 2 tbsp salad dressing (e.g., vinaigrette or ranch)

Instructions:

  1. Combine all the vegetables in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with salad dressing and toss well.

4. Microwave Mug Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped vegetables, cheese, or cooked meat

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a microwave-safe mug, add milk, and whisk.
  2. Stir in optional ingredients.
  3. Microwave for 1-2 minutes, stopping every 30 seconds to stir.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
